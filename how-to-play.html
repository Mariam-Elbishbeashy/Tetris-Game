How to Play Tetris

Game Basics

Tetris is a tile-matching puzzle game where you arrange falling blocks called "tetrominoes" to create complete horizontal lines.

Objective

Rotate and move the falling tetrominoes to create complete horizontal lines without gaps. When a line is completed, it disappears and you earn points.

Controls

Movement

Use ← and → arrow keys to move the piece left or right.

On mobile: Swipe left or right on the screen.

Rotation

Press ↑ to rotate the piece clockwise.

On mobile: Swipe up or tap the rotate button.

Falling Speed

Press ↓ to make the piece fall faster.

Scoring

Points System

  • • 1 line: 1000 points
  • • 1 shape: 10 points
  • • 1 Press ↓: 1 point

Game Over

The game ends when the stacked tetrominoes reach the top of the playing field. Try to beat your high score!
